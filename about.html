Our Journey

True Worshippers Continental was founded on July 20, 2020. It began as a fellowship in the Soul Clinic Community, Montserrado County, Liberia. Shortly after, the Holy Spirit ministered to His servant, calling for the establishment of a sanctuary that would stand as a beacon of hope for salvation and deliverance.

Following divine guidance, the church relocated to YMCA Community, G.S.A Road, Paynesville City, Liberia. This holy sanctuary is devoted to setting the captives free through Christ, whom we serve faithfully. The power of God moves within this ministry like a tsunami, bringing transformation, healing, and breakthroughs to all who come to worship in truth and spirit.
